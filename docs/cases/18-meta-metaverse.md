## Overview

Facebook renamed itself Meta and spent heavily on metaverse and VR initiatives while investors questioned adoption, timing, and founder control.

## Management Problem

How long should a company fund a strategic bet before evidence of adoption arrives?

## Why It Matters

Meta lets students debate patient innovation investment versus unchecked founder-controlled spending.

## Debate Question

> When should companies abandon big bets, and should shareholders be able to override founders?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Dual-class control gives Zuckerberg unusual persistence.
- Reality Labs losses test investor patience.
- The pivot reflects both strategic foresight and market-timing risk.
