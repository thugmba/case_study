## Overview

Delta Electronics transitioned from a B2B component supplier of switching power supplies to an integrated industrial system solution provider in smart automation, EV charging, and green energy.

## Management Problem

Should component vendors take the risk of offering integrated systems, potentially competing with system-integrator customers?

## Why It Matters

It illustrates the path of component manufacturers migrating up the value chain to capture solution premiums.

## Debate Question

> Should component vendors take the risk of offering integrated systems, potentially competing with system-integrator customers?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Integrated solutions (like EV chargers) command higher margins than components.
- System integration requires building direct relations with corporate and municipal end-users.
- Offering systems can create channel conflict with existing OEM component buyers.
