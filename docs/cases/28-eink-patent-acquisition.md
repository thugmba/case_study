## Overview

E Ink acquired its key technology and patent portfolio from MIT spin-off E Ink Corporation, pivoting from LCD panel manufacturing to dominate the global electronic paper display market.

## Management Problem

Is buying intellectual property more sustainable than organic R&D for dominating high-tech markets?

## Why It Matters

It shows how strategic M&A and patent control can build a defensible technological monopoly.

## Debate Question

> Is buying intellectual property more sustainable than organic R&D for dominating high-tech markets?

## Evidence to Discuss

- E Ink holds a dominant market share in global electronic paper displays.
- Patent acquisition protected the company from fast follower clones.
- Expanding display applications to retail ESL and smart cities sustained growth beyond e-readers.
