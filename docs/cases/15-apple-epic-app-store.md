## Overview

Epic challenged Apple's App Store commission and payment restrictions, raising antitrust questions about platform power and app-store governance.

## Management Problem

Is a 30% commission fair compensation for platform access, or an abuse of gatekeeper power?

## Why It Matters

The dispute shows how platform rules can be both quality controls and value-capture mechanisms.

## Debate Question

> Is 30% fair for platform access, and should app stores be regulated as utilities?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Apple provides distribution, trust, and payment infrastructure.
- Developers argue that payment restrictions limit competition.
- The case illustrates cross-side effects between users and developers.
