## Overview

Airbnb faced customer frustration over low nightly prices that rose sharply at checkout because of cleaning and service fees.

## Management Problem

Should platforms mandate all-in pricing even when hosts set many of the fees?

## Why It Matters

Airbnb shows how platform design can make technically legal fees feel unfair and damage trust.

## Debate Question

> Should platforms mandate all-in pricing, and who is responsible: platform or hosts?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Drip pricing changes perceived fairness.
- Hosts and platform rules jointly produce the customer experience.
- Transparency can improve trust while reducing short-term click appeal.
