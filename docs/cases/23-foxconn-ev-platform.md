## Overview

Foxconn transitioned from consumer electronics assembly to the electric vehicle market, establishing the open-source MIH platform to modularize EV design and manufacturing.

## Management Problem

Can open-source platforms successfully commoditize contract manufacturing in the automotive sector?

## Why It Matters

It tests whether smartphone-style modular manufacturing can succeed in the asset-heavy automotive industry.

## Debate Question

> Can open-source platforms successfully commoditize contract manufacturing in the automotive sector?

## Evidence to Discuss

- MIH open-source platform aims to reduce EV design and manufacturing cycles.
- Automotive safety regulations and branding differ from consumer hardware.
- Traditional automotive giants maintain proprietary control over their hardware.
