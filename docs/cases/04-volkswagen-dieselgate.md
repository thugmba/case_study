## Overview

Volkswagen installed software that cheated diesel-emissions tests, affecting millions of vehicles and triggering fines, executive exits, prosecutions, and industry-wide consequences.

## Management Problem

How should responsibility be assigned when misconduct is embedded in technical systems and organizational pressure?

## Why It Matters

Dieselgate connects individual choices, engineering work, environmental claims, and corporate incentives.

## Debate Question

> Who is responsible: engineers who coded it, managers who ordered it, or executives who created pressure?

## Evidence to Discuss

- The defeat device was technical, but the incentives were organizational.
- Environmental positioning intensified the trust damage.
- The case helps distinguish legal accountability from managerial responsibility.
