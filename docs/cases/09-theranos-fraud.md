## Overview

Theranos raised hundreds of millions of dollars while overstating blood-testing capabilities that did not reliably work as represented.

## Management Problem

Where is the line between founder vision and fraud?

## Why It Matters

Theranos is useful because investors, directors, partners, employees, regulators, and patients all had different information and risks.

## Debate Question

> What is the line between vision and fraud, and why did experts fail to see through it?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Secrecy limited technical validation.
- Prestigious board members did not substitute for domain expertise.
- Patients turned investor fraud into a public-health issue.
