## Overview

MediaTek launched its Dimensity processor series to shift from entry-level and mid-range mobile markets to direct competition with Qualcomm in the premium mobile chip segment.

## Management Problem

Can a high-volume, low-cost component manufacturer successfully reposition its brand to the premium segment?

## Why It Matters

It illustrates the strategic and branding challenges of moving a brand upmarket.

## Debate Question

> Can a high-volume, low-cost component manufacturer successfully reposition its brand to the premium segment?

## Evidence to Discuss

- MediaTek's Dimensity chips achieved competitive parity in raw benchmarks.
- Premium OEMs still hold high affinity for Qualcomm's brand equity.
- The transition requires high upfront research investments and premium marketing.
