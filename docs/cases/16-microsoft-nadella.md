## Overview

Satya Nadella shifted Microsoft from internal rivalry and Windows defensiveness toward cloud, learning culture, and cross-platform strategy.

## Management Problem

Can culture change drive financial and strategic renewal?

## Why It Matters

Microsoft provides a rare large-company example where culture, strategy, and financial performance changed together.

## Debate Question

> Can culture really drive financial results, and what did Nadella do differently?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Cloud strategy required cross-unit collaboration.
- A learning culture reduced defensive behavior.
- Leadership language changed expectations about growth and mistakes.
