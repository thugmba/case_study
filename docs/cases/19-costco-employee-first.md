## Overview

Costco pays above many retail competitors and accepts Wall Street criticism while arguing that low turnover and operating discipline support performance.

## Management Problem

Can higher wages be an efficiency strategy rather than only a cost?

## Why It Matters

Costco challenges the assumption that retail efficiency requires minimizing labor cost.

## Debate Question

> Is Costco's model replicable, and why do not more companies copy it?

## Evidence to Discuss

- Lower turnover can reduce hidden operating costs.
- Employee investment works with disciplined process design.
- The case connects stakeholder treatment to productivity.
