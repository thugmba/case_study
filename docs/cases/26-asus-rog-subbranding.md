## Overview

ASUS established the Republic of Gamers (ROG) sub-brand to escape PC margin dilution and build a premium ecosystem across gaming hardware, mobile, and accessories.

## Management Problem

Is sub-branding or corporate branding more effective for sustaining growth in declining hardware sectors?

## Why It Matters

It shows how to use sub-branding to capture high-margin segments in commodity markets.

## Debate Question

> Is sub-branding or corporate branding more effective for sustaining growth in declining hardware sectors?

## Evidence to Discuss

- ROG sub-brand commands premium prices over standard ASUS components.
- The gaming community values distinct ROG aesthetics and performance branding.
- Sub-branding allowed ASUS to experiment with premium accessories without damaging the core brand utility.
